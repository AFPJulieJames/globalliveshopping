Why live shopping matters

Live Commerce is Reshaping How the World Shops

Live shopping has moved from a trend to a $20 billion global industry in under five years. What started in Asia with platforms like Taobao Live has exploded globally through TikTok Shop, Amazon Live, and Instagram Live shopping.

Brands are shifting marketing budgets from traditional advertising to live shopping affiliate programs. Creators are replacing brand deals with commission-based live selling. And consumers are buying more from live streams than from static product pages.

North America Leader
TikTok Shop

The fastest growing live shopping platform in the United States. TikTok Shop has rapidly become the dominant live commerce channel for creator affiliates, with commission rates up to 20% and affiliate link engagement rates 160% higher than Instagram.

Established Leader
Amazon Live

Amazon's live shopping program connects creators with the world's largest product catalog. Amazon Live affiliate commissions through the Associates program make it one of the most credible and accessible live shopping affiliate channels globally.

Social Commerce
Instagram Live Shopping

Meta's Instagram Live shopping integration reaches over 100 million active social shoppers. Brands including Sephora, GAP, and American Eagle have launched Instagram affiliate programs specifically targeting live shopping creators.

Long Form Commerce
YouTube Live Shopping

YouTube Live shopping combines long form content trust with real time purchasing. YouTube creators can stack affiliate commissions from Google's program with direct brand deals for maximum live commerce income.

Collectibles Market
Whatnot

Whatnot is the leading live shopping marketplace for collectibles, trading cards, vintage fashion, and unique finds. A distinct corner of the global live commerce economy with loyal creator communities and strong repeat purchase behavior.

Global Origin

Live shopping originated in China with Taobao Live and has spread globally through platforms including Douyin, Shopee Live, and Lazada Live. GlobalLiveShopping.com tracks how Asian live commerce innovations are influencing the global market.
